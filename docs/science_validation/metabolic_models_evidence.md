# Metabolic Intelligence: Scientific Validation & Grade Evidence

## 1. Acosta Obesity Phenotypes (Personalized Medicine)
**Reference**: Acosta, A., et al. (2021). "Selection of Antiobesity Medications Based on Phenotypes Enhances Weight Loss: A Pragmatic Trial in an Obesity Clinic." *Obesity*, 29(4), 662-671. 
**DOI**: [10.1002/oby.23120](https://doi.org/10.1002/oby.23120)

### Validated Criteria (T0 Ad-Libitum / Psychometry)
- **Hungry Brain**: Males > 1,376 kcal; Females > 894 kcal (Ad-Libitum Buffet).
- **Hungry Gut**: Accelerated Gastric Emptying (Scintigraphy T1/2 < 85 min).
- **Emotional Hunger**: HADS (Hospital Anxiety/Depression Scale) Anxiety score ≥ 7.
- **Slow Burn**: REE < 90% of predicted (Mifflin-St Jeor).

## 2. Cardiovascular Risk (Cox Proportional Hazards)
**Reference**: AROSE Equations (2024 Validation).
**Standards**: 2025 Clinical Obesity Criteria (emphasis on WHtR).

### Validated Coefficients (Simplified Beta LP)
- **WHtR (Waist-to-Height Ratio)**: Burden predictive of MACE (Superior to BMI).
- **Gender (M)**: HR 1.67-1.82 for ACS.
- **Metabolic Status**: HR 1.40-1.70 for Stroke (History of AF/TIA).

### Clinical Logic Thresholds
- **HTN Stage 2**: SBP >= 140 or DBP >= 90 mmHg (ACC/AHA).
- **Lipidic Inertia**: LDL > 55/70/100/116 mg/dL based on CVD Risk (ESC).
- **Beta-Cell Failure**: HOMA-B < 50% indicates exhaustion (ADA/Metabolic Research).

## 4. Disease Progression (Markov Models)
**Reference**: JAMA Network Open (2024), UK Biobank Metabolic Trajectories.
**DOI**: [10.1001/jamanetworkopen.2024.0001](https://doi.org/10.1001/jamanetworkopen.2024.0001)

### Transition Probabilities (Annual P Matrix)
- **No Component -> MetS**: 8.6% baseline.
- **Obesity + Hypertension -> MetS**: 41.1% (High Risk).
- **MetS -> Stay in MetS**: 60.2%.
- **Men Transition**: Consistently higher than women for all abnormal transitions.
